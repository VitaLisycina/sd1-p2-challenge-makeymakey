# Deliverables
## Main video
- The video should clearly show the project
- Start by explaining what the project is about
- Show how the project works (demo your project)
  - During the demo, explain what you're doing.
- Finish with a conclusion
  - what went well when working on this project?
  - what didn't go well?
  - what would you do differently next time?

## Demo video
- Show how someone interacts with your physical prototype.

## Photo's
- Make photo's of someone interacting with your prototype.
